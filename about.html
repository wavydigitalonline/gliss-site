About

Precision as a discipline.

Gliss is a studio built for businesses that value quality and speed in equal measure. We combine premium design, disciplined marketing and modern AI to help brands operate with clarity and grow with confidence.

Every project we ship — from a one-page website to a fully automated customer journey — starts with the same question: where is the friction? Then we remove it.

We keep our team small on purpose. It means the person designing your website is the same person tuning your ads and the same person deploying your AI. No hand-offs. No layers. Just measurable progress.

The result is work that feels effortless — because behind the scenes, every detail is engineered to be inevitable.

Meet the Founder

Built from the ground up.

I'm Kevin, the founder of Gliss.

I didn't start this agency with funding, an office, or expensive equipment. I started it on a Xiaomi Redmi phone while working 10-hour shifts, building websites during breaks and late into the night.

That experience taught me something most agencies never have to learn: every click matters, every enquiry matters, and every rand a business invests should create measurable value.

Gliss was built on one belief: growing a business shouldn't feel complicated. The best digital experiences are smooth, effortless, and almost invisible. When everything works as it should, your customers don't notice the technology—they simply take the next step.

Today, that same mindset shapes every project we deliver — thoughtful design, practical solutions, and technology that helps businesses grow with confidence.
